Since growing a beard I've gotten in the habit of making my own beard oil. It is a lot cheaper in the long run and plus, it's fun to make. I use a blend of hemp seed oil and jojoba oil (~2:1 ratio) for the carrier oil.

I decided to make a larger batch this time around. This was partially because my primary graduated flask only goes down to 40 mL. It just so happens that I only had 40 mL of jojoba oil left anyway, so it was a perfect fit. I then added 80 mL of hemp seed oil.

For scent, I used a blend of essential oils. For this batch I went with lemon, patchouli, cinnamon, and graperuit. I used 225 drops of lemon oil, 30 drops patchouli, 10 drops cinnamon, and 25 drops grapefruit. I only added the grapefruit because the 225 drops of lemon still wasn't quite enough to give the desired citrus scent (which seems a bit ridiculous, but I added it incrementally and kept testing the oil). I think part of the issue is that hemp seed oil has a fairly strong scent by itself and so it takes quite a lot to cut through.

Overall, I'm fairly happy with the blend, but it could still be a little stronger. Perhaps I'll wait until I've gone through some of it and then add more scent later.
